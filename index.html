    Biography

    Yulei Wang received his Ph.D. in Computer Technology and Application from Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST), Macau, China, in Feb., 2024, M.S. degree in Computer and Information Systems from MUST in Aug., 2019, and B.S. degree in Information and Computing Science from University of Science and Technology Beijing, Beijing, China, in 2015.

    His research interests include wireless networks, semantic communication and stochastic geometry.
